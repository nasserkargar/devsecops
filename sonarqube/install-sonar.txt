Analyzing a Project
-------------------
Now that you're logged in to your local SonarQube instance, let's analyze a project:

    Click the Create new project button.
    Give your project a Project key and a Display name and click the Set Up button.
    Under Provide a token, select Generate a token. Give your token a name, click the Generate button, 
	and click Continue.
    Select your project's main language under Run analysis on your project, and follow the instructions 
	to analyze your project.
